CAPA GRADUATING CLASS OF 2022

Documentary Film

This documentary captures the experiences and reflections of the Philadelphia High School for the Creative & Performing Arts (CAPA)'s graduating class of 2022 via 22 in-depth interviews. Filmed, directed, edited, and produced independently, the project highlights each student’s personal journey, celebrating their achievements and aspirations.

please stand by.

Experimental Film

please stand by. is an experimental film that delves into the interplay of color, aspect ratio, and unconventional composition. This project challenges visual norms, using shifting perspectives and bold color contrasts to create a unique viewing experience that elicits emotion and invites them into an exploration of visual storytelling.

LA SALLE TV

Producer / Host

La Salle TV is a student-run television program that provides hands-on experience both on and off camera. My key contributions include hosting and producing Backstage Pass, one of the program’s main shows, as well as Just Next Door. Additional work includes creating graphics for both shows, showcasing versatility in on-camera presence, behind-the-scenes production, and visual storytelling.
