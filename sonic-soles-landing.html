OVERVIEW

Sonic Soles is a duo collaboration project focused on creating a whimsical yet “useless” gadget, accompanied by a compelling promotional campaign. The aim of the project is to make the concept engaging and visually appealing, transforming the absurdity of the gadget into an enticing and imaginative experience that captures attention and sparks curiosity.



Designed in: Adobe Photoshop 2024.
