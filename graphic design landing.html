Apparel Design

Infused with coastal charm, Sandy Jawn brings breezy, beach-inspired visuals to summer apparel. Soft waves, sun-washed colors, and relaxed typography capture the essence of summer in a stylish vintage, everyday design.

MANDALA PORTRAITS

Series Design

The Mandala Portrait series explores color theory and client collaboration through three unique designs, each tailored to specific brand guidelines and aesthetic requests. FEED THE BEAST POSTER DESIGN

Personal Project

This poster design for Kim Petras' Feed The Beast album is a personal project exploring texture and bold typography. Layered visuals and tactile elements capture the album’s edgy energy, merging experimental textures with dynamic type to create a striking, immersive effect.

LETTERLOOT

Collaborative Project

Letter Loot is a collaborative project centered on designing a fictional board game from scratch. Developed as a group exploration of teamwork and creativity, this project included every element of the game—name, rules, board layout, game pieces, and card design. The result is a cohesive and playful concept showcasing the power of collaborative design

SONIC SOLES

Collaborative Project

Sonic Soles is a duo collaboration project focused on creating a whimsical yet “useless” gadget, accompanied by a compelling promotional campaign. The aim of the project is to make the concept engaging and visually appealing, transforming the absurdity of the gadget into an enticing and imaginative experience that captures attention and sparks curiosity

BACKSTAGE ASKS LOGO DESIGN
